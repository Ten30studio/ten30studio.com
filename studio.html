Why Ten30 exists.

Ten30 Studio started in Arlington, Texas in 2024. One person, one workspace, one idea: make planners, journals, and AI tools that feel like they were made on purpose, not assembled from a template.

Every product on the shelf is something we use ourselves. Every AI build is something we'd ship for our own business first. That's the whole bet: make it good enough that we'd use it, then put it within reach.

We sell on Etsy and Gumroad because that's where buyers are. We build AI systems for operators who want production-grade work, not strategy decks. We keep our prices honest and our scope tight, because we'd rather make 23 things well than 230 things in a hurry.

01. How we work

Principle 01

We use what we make.

If we wouldn't pull it off our own shelf, we don't put it on yours. Every planner, every template, every agent system gets dogfooded inside Ten30 first.

Principle 02

We ship the thing.

Decks don't move businesses. Working systems do. Every engagement ends with you holding the keys, not a 60-slide handover.

Principle 03

We stay small on purpose.

Small means we can answer email ourselves, ship in a week, and care about a $5 planner the same way we care about a $25k engagement.
