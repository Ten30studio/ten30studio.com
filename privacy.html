Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 May 2026 · Ten30 LLC, Arlington, Texas

This policy explains what information Ten30 Studios ("Ten30", "we", "us") collects when you use ten30studio.com and its sub-pages, why we collect it, and the choices you have. We keep this short and plain.

Information you give us

We collect information only when you choose to send it:

  • CareFlow assessment forms. If you submit a CareFlow enquiry, we collect the details you enter — type of care needed, funding type, hours, preferred start date, and your contact details — so we can respond to your enquiry.
  • Email. When you email us, we receive your address and whatever you write.
  • Purchases. Payments for AI services are processed by Stripe. We receive confirmation of a completed payment; we do not store your full card details.

Information collected automatically

Like most websites, we use Google Analytics and Google Ads conversion tracking. These set cookies and collect standard analytics data — pages visited, approximate location, device and browser type, and referral source — to help us understand traffic and measure advertising. This data is processed by Google under its own privacy terms.

How we use your information

  • To respond to enquiries and deliver the products or services you request
  • To process payments and provide receipts
  • To measure and improve our website and advertising

We do not sell your personal information.

Who we share it with

We share information only with service providers that help us run the business — currently Stripe (payments), Google (analytics and advertising), and our email provider — and only as needed to provide the service. We may also disclose information if required by law.

CareFlow enquiries

CareFlow enquiries can include health- and care-related details. We treat this information as confidential, use it only to respond to the enquiry, and retain it only as long as needed for that purpose. If you are in the UK or EU, you have rights over this data under the UK GDPR / GDPR — see "Your choices" below.

Your choices

  • You can ask us what personal information we hold about you, ask for a copy, or ask us to delete it — email admin@ten30studio.com.
  • You can opt out of Google Analytics with the Google opt-out add-on, and manage ad personalisation in your Google account.
  • You can block or clear cookies in your browser settings.

Retention & security

We keep personal information only as long as needed for the purpose it was collected, then delete it. We take reasonable measures to protect it, but no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Children

Our website and services are not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

Changes

We may update this policy. The "last updated" date above shows when it last changed.

Contact

Questions about privacy: admin@ten30studio.com.

Draft notice: this is a working privacy policy drafted by Ten30 to reflect how the site currently operates. It is a starting point, not legal advice — have it reviewed by a qualified attorney before relying on it for compliance.
