The Problem

Every small business answers the same questions every day.

A barbershop in Texas, a boutique in London, a food vendor in Lagos — they all type the same answers, all day, every day. After hours, customers go unserved. There is no leverage, no scale, no rest. Existing tools fail them by demanding the one thing they don't have: time to learn software.

  • After-hours blindspot. Customers inquire at night. Owner sleeps. Customer moves on.
  • Repetition fatigue. The same answers, typed 30 times a day. Kills productivity.
  • No scale without headcount. More customers = more manual responses. Linear cost.
  • Trust gap with dashboards. Dashboard products get abandoned. Owners don't trust software they weren't born with.
  • Cost barrier. Enterprise chatbots start at $200–$500/month. That's rent money.

The Solution

Two agents. One WhatsApp account. Zero apps to learn.

FMIFY meets the SMB owner where they already live. They chat updates into WhatsApp the same way they chat with their bookkeeper or their staff. A second agent — named by them, branded by them — handles every customer who messages the business.

Why Now

Three curves crossed. They didn't cross 24 months ago.

1

WhatsApp Business API matured.

Messaging cost dropped ~80% since 2022. A 1,000-msg/month business now runs at $10–15 infra cost — viable at SMB price points.

2

AI inference got cheap enough.

GPT-4-class intelligence at ~$0.003–$0.010 per 1K tokens. A full customer Q&A interaction costs under half a cent. The unit economics now work below $25/month price points.

3

WhatsApp is now mainstream globally.

2B+ active users. Fastest-growing messaging channel in the US. The "will they use it" question is settled — businesses and customers are already there. Nobody's built the SMB customer service layer on top of it.

A fourth tailwind: no incumbent has solved this for SMBs. The category is fragmented — helpdesk tools, messaging APIs, generic chatbot builders — and every player demands the one thing the customer won't give: time to learn software.
